Travel

For our friends travelling from outside Southampton we've prepared some travel suggestions. Get in touch if you would like more information - we've tried all of these at various times!

From Heathrow Airport

The simplest way is to take a National Express bus which originates at the airport and is about a 2 hour trip. Check out NationalExpress.com to plan or book your journey - your start location will be Heathrow Airport (you can find your terminal here) and you can travel to either Southampton Coach Station (closest to the city) or University of Southampton (closest to the wedding).

You can also take a train to Southampton which will involve several transfers. Check out nationalrail.co.uk for train times and booking (speak to us if you need help!)

From Central London

The train out of London Waterloo station takes a little over an hour to reach Southampton and direct trains are available. You can arrive at either Southampton Central (slightly easier) or Southampton Airport Parkway and get a UniLink bus to wherever you're staying. Check out nationalrail.co.uk for train times and booking (speak to us if you need help!)

You can also get a National Express coach from Victoria coach station

Around Southampton

Southampton has a great bus system called UniLink, many taxis, and is very walkable. For our American guests, unless you feel the need to drive on the opposite side of the road, you can probably manage without renting a car.
